About David

David graduated from University of Michigan with a B.S. in Industrial & Operations Engineering in 2017. Since graduating, his interest in data analytics brought him around the world and back.

David currently works in Seattle, WA for Mu Sigma Inc. - a category defining decision sciences and big data analytics company, helping enterprises systematize better data-driven decision making.

David is an adventurous traveler, coffee enthusiast, soccer player, football fan,   and loyal friend.
